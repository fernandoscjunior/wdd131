What is Terere

Terere or cold Mate is a cold drink made with crushed mate leaves called erva-mate (Ilex paraguariensis) and cold water or juice (sometimes there are other leaves to inhence the flavour) . It is very popular in some regions of South America being widely consumed in Paraguay, Brazil, Argentina, and Bolivia, it is more than just a drink though, it is a form of culture and expressing yourself, it helps you make new friends and be part of a community.
